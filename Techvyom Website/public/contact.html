Frequently Asked Questions

What is TECHVYOM 2025?
TECHVYOM 2025 is an annual technical festival organized by SRMS College of Engineering and Technology. It features various technical competitions, workshops, and events across different clubs including Illuminati Club, Equinox Club, Synergy Club, Robotrax Club, Aeronautics Club, Literary Club, Pharmquimica Club, and more.
How can I register for events?
You can register through our website using the 'Registration' page for individual events or the 'Team Registration' page for team events. Fill out the required details and select the events you're interested in participating. You'll receive a unique Participant ID (PID) for individual registration or Team ID for team registrations.
Is there a registration fee for the events?
Registration fees vary by event. Some events may be free, while others might have a nominal fee. The fee details are mentioned in the description of each event on the Events page. Payment information will be provided after registration.
Can I participate in multiple events?
Yes, you can register for multiple events, but make sure to check the event schedule to avoid time conflicts. Select all the events you wish to participate in during the registration process. Our system allows you to manage your participation across various club events.
How do I know if my registration is confirmed?
After successful registration, you will receive a confirmation page with your unique Participant ID (PID) or Team ID, which you should save for future reference. You can also contact the organizers if you need to verify your registration status or make changes to your event selections.
